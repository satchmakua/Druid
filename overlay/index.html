The volunteer rescue ecosystem has archived far more than Verderer observes. This index cross-references those third-party archives (wayback) against Verderer's own attested record. A verderer-attested resource carries a self-verifying proof bundle — exactly-these-bytes, offline-verifiable, trusting no one. A third-party-only copy is a real archive, but there is nothing to prove about its bytes, so it shows no badge.
